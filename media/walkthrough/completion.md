## Write COBOL faster

- **Code completion** -- variables, paragraphs/sections (offered first after `PERFORM` / `GO TO`) and common COBOL keywords, all from the current program's symbol index.
- **Copybook autocomplete** -- type `COPY ` to pick a copybook from your configured folders.
- **Snippets** -- ready-made templates for `IF`, `EVALUATE`, `PERFORM`, `CALL`, parametric `PIC` clauses, `FD`/`SELECT` and a full program skeleton.

Trigger any of these with `Ctrl+Space`.

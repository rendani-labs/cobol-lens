## Read nested logic at a glance

Place the cursor on an `IF`, `ELSE` or `END-IF` line and COBOL Lens highlights the matching block with colored keyword borders.

**Scope bars** along the left margin track every nesting level (up to 9), so even deeply nested conditions stay readable -- a feature you will not find in most COBOL extensions.

Toggle it with `cobolLens.ifBlockHighlight.enabled` and `cobolLens.ifBlockHighlight.scopeBars`.
